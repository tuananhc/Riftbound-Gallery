Mark your calendars. The **Riftbound Spring Invitational 2026** will take place on **April 26–27**, and qualification is now open to all ranked players.

## Format

This year's Invitational uses a revised competitive structure:

- **Day 1:** 8 rounds of Swiss (Standard Constructed, Set 3 legal)
- **Day 2:** Top 8 single-elimination playoff
- **Deck registration:** 60-card main deck, 15-card sideboard

## Prize Pool

| Placement | Prize |
|-----------|-------|
| 1st       | $4,000 |
| 2nd       | $2,000 |
| 3rd–4th   | $1,000 each |
| 5th–8th   | $250 each |

## How to Qualify

Qualification runs through three pathways:

1. **Ranked ladder** — Top 200 players on the Diamond+ leaderboard as of April 19 receive automatic invites
2. **Open qualifiers** — Weekly online qualifier events run every Saturday through April 19
3. **Direct invite** — Previous Invitational finalists receive standing invitations

Registration for the first qualifier opens this Saturday. Good luck, and may the Rift be with you.
