The wait is over. **Echoes of the Shattered Rift** is now live across all platforms, bringing the largest card set in Riftbound history.

## What's in the Set?

180 new cards span all six color identities, with a particular focus on multi-color synergies that shake up the existing meta. Highlights include:

- **12 new Legendary cards** — one for each pure color and six for two-color pairs
- **The Echo mechanic** — when an Echo card enters play, you may copy its effect if you cast another spell this turn
- **Fracture spells** — split cards that can be cast as either half, or both halves at increased cost

## The Rift Walker Returns

Set 2's most beloved archetype — the Arcane/Shadow Rift Walker shell — receives major support with five new cards directly referencing the card type. Competitive players have already begun theorycrafting, and early results from the Set 3 preview events look promising.

## Balance Changes

Alongside the new set, we've issued balance patches for **seven existing cards** that had become dominant in the post-Set 2 meta. Full details are in the patch notes.
